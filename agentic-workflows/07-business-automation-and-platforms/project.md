# Project — Automation Service Pack

## Goal

Design a small but believable automation offering you could sell to an internal team or an external client.

## Deliverables

1. One **platform recommendation memo** explaining why you chose your stack
2. One implemented workflow in your chosen platform
3. One supporting system integration such as CRM, database, messaging, or billing
4. One simple runbook for failures and retries
5. One short pricing or packaging note explaining how the work could be offered as a service

## Constraints

- the workflow must solve a real business process, not just a toy chatbot
- the platform recommendation must compare at least two alternatives honestly
- one section must explain what happens when an API fails or a step times out
- one section must say what would make you move off the initial platform later

## Done when

- another engineer or operator could understand the flow without a live walkthrough
- the system boundary is explicit
- the delivery model is realistic enough that someone could imagine paying for it
