## Step order for each topic

1. **read** — work through `README.md` until you can explain the concept without looking at it
2. **demo** — run `bash demo.sh` and understand each command; re-run with variations to build intuition
3. **implement** — complete `homework.md`, saving your work to `progress/<date>/working-folder/containers/`

## How to advance

Within a topic: bump `Next step`: `read` → `demo` → `implement`.
After `implement` finishes: increment `Topic index in level` and reset `Next step` to `read`. Update `Topic` and `Topic path` from the syllabus.
After the last topic in a stage: bump `Level` and reset `Topic index in level` to 1.
